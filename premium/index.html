Metalama Editions

Metalama follows an open core business model: most of its features (and not just the core ones) are free and open source, while convenience features and extension packages are offered under a commercial license. If you're using Metalama for serious work in a corporate environment, we recommend a Metalama Enterprise subscription. It supports the long-term sustainability of the project and gives you access to all features and our highest level of support.
